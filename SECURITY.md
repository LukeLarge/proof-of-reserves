## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability, please use the "Report a vulnerability" feature on GitHub. This can be found under the "Security" tab of our repository.

Alternatively, you can email us at [SECURITY_CONTACT@example.com].

We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.
